A school project, became serious

Leapkit started as a school project in August 2013, on the leading Business School in Denmark, Copenhagen Business School. The idea was received with such enthusiasm by teachers and the university in general, we decided to reach out to other universities and hear if they were equally interested. The overall response was extremely positive, so we continued working on the project, now as a real company, after the school project officially ended.

So, based on our experiences and our research we started thinking about this problem could be solved. We ended up with a platform that should work like a matchmaking site, this time for companies searching students to engage in their projects and vice versa. We also wanted the element of projects across universities so students from different fields could work together on projects and complement each other skills... And Leapkit was born

Our mission

We have taken the responsibilities to integrate realistic and practical projects in higher education in the easiest and simplest way. Our mission is to increase the possibility for students to work with companies and students from different fields and thereby contributing to a better education.

What is Leapkit?

At Leapkit you can search for students to participate in your school projects, as well as search through school projects that you can contribute in! In other words, Leapkit gives you an opportunity to join forces with students from different fields, thereby placing your school projects in another dimension. With this approach your project becomes a kick-ass experience with a kick-ass outcome! It most likely leads to your first entrepreneurial leap!

Another possibility on Leapkit is a search of projects uploaded by companies. Companies are searching for students to solve cases and projects like never before. Instead of doing a school product regarding a made-up scene, you can use an actual case from an actual company. And who knows - they might hire you based on the case you just solved..
